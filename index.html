UCP

Universal E-commerce Protocol

What it is: A standardized data layer that allows AI agents, platforms, and merchants to exchange E-commerce information consistently.

  • Enables AI agents to discover and compare products across sellers
  • Standardizes product feeds, pricing, inventory, and shipping
  • Reduces dependency on custom integrations or scraping
ACP

Agentic E-commerce Protocol

What it is: A transaction execution framework that allows AI agents to take E-commerce actions safely and correctly.

  • Handles purchase execution and order confirmations
  • Manages permissions, authentication, and user consent
  • Supports post-purchase actions like tracking and refunds

How to Implement Agentic AI

A strategic roadmap for E-commerce transformation.

1

Identify High-Impact Areas

Focus on customer engagement, inventory, and fraud prevention. virtual try-ons and personalized recommendations offer immediate ROI.

2

Develop a Clear AI Strategy

Align integration with business goals like revenue growth or operational efficiency. Establish clear KPIs to measure success.

3

Choose the Right Tech Stack

Utilize frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch. Deploy on AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure. Integrate with existing systems via REST APIs.

4

Pilot Projects Before Deployment

Start small (e.g., a single chatbot) and set measurable goals. Analyze performance and iterate before scaling.
